Veil Stack (Canteen)

Decentralized container orchestrator on FEVM + libp2p

Veil Stack replaces the Kubernetes control plane with a smart contract on FEVM and a libp2p peer-to-peer mesh. Every workload scheduled through Veil Stack originates a paid Filecoin storage deal anchored on-chain.
